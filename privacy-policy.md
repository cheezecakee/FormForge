# Privacy Policy for Form Forge

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Overview

Form Forge is a Chrome extension that helps users fill job application forms by storing and reusing their personal and professional information. This policy explains how Form Forge handles your data.

## Data Collection

Form Forge does **not** collect, transmit, or share any of your data. All information you enter is stored exclusively on your device using Chrome's local storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).

## What is stored

When you use Form Forge, the following information may be saved locally on your device:

- Personal details (name, date of birth, gender, race, citizenship)
- Contact information (email, phone number)
- Home and permanent addresses
- Work history
- Education history
- Identification documents (passport number, national ID, etc.)
- Legal status information (work authorization, disability, veteran status)
- Links (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio, etc.)
- Languages and skills
- Salary expectations
- Cover letters
- FAQ answers
- A list of websites where Form Forge is enabled

This data never leaves your device. It is never sent to any server, third party, or external service.

## How your data is used

Your stored information is used solely to populate the Form Forge side panel when you visit a supported job application website, allowing you to copy or fill form fields faster.

## Data sharing

Form Forge does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party under any circumstances.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is stored locally until you choose to delete it. You can clear your entire profile at any time using the **Clear Profile** button in the extension. Uninstalling the extension will also remove all locally stored data.

## Permissions

Form Forge requests the following Chrome permissions:

- **storage** — to save your profile and site list locally on your device
- **tabs** — to detect whether the current page is a supported job site
- **activeTab** — to access the currently active tab when you use the popup
- **scripting** — to inject the Form Forge panel into job application pages
- **host permissions** — to run the extension on supported job application websites

No permission is used to collect or transmit data.

## Third-party services

Form Forge does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Children's privacy

Form Forge is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
