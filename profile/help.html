Applying to Jobs

When visiting a supported job website, Forge can help fill application fields using the information saved in your profile.

1
Open the Forge panel from the job application page.

The panel can also open when interacting with a supported input field. Selecting a field allows Forge to know where information should be inserted.

1
Select a supported application field. Forge remembers this selection while filling the form.
2
Choose information from the panel to fill the selected field. If no field is selected, the information is copied instead.
3
Notifications show whether the action succeeded, failed, or copied the information instead.
4
Shows the currently selected field or indicates that no field is selected.
5
Close the Forge panel.

Some saved information, such as cover letters or FAQ answers, can be selected before inserting.

1
Select saved content from the dropdown.
2
Insert the selected information into the application.
Tips & Support

Tips for getting the best results with Forge.

  • Keep your profile updated so Forge has the latest information available.
  • Select the relevant information before copying or inserting it into an application.
  • If a field cannot be filled automatically, Forge will copy the information instead.
